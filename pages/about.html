About

Your Trusted Pittsburgh Gold Buyer

Shawn Logan brings years of experience from running a successful Pittsburgh pawn shop. He’s helped hundreds of customers sell their valuables safely and get paid fairly. No middlemen, just expert evaluations and fast payouts.

Tired of the lowball offers and impersonal service of typical pawn shops, Shawn decided to create a better way for people to sell their precious metals. At Shawn's Gold Standard, you deal directly with the expert. This means you get a more accurate evaluation, a higher payout, and a private, professional experience every time.

Whether you're selling old jewelry, inherited coins, or scrap gold, Shawn is committed to making the process transparent, simple, and rewarding for you.

“My goal is to treat every customer with respect. I built this business on fairness and transparency, and that’s a promise I stand by.”
– Shawn Logan
