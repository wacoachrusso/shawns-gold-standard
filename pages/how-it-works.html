A Simple & Secure Process

We've streamlined selling your precious metals to be as easy and transparent as possible. Here’s how it works.

Step 1

Submit Your Items Online

Start by filling out our secure quote form. Provide a brief description of your items and upload a few photos. It’s quick, easy, and can be done from any device.

Step 2

Receive a Fair Offer

Shawn will personally review your submission and provide a top-dollar offer based on current market values. We pride ourselves on transparency—no hidden fees, just an honest price.

Step 3

Secure Your Items

Once you accept, we arrange the logistics. You can choose to meet at a secure public location in the Pittsburgh area or use our free, fully insured, and prepaid shipping kit to send us your items.

Step 4

Get Paid Instantly

The moment we verify your items, your payment is processed immediately. Choose the method that works best for you: Cash, Zelle, Venmo, or a business check.
