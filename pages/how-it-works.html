Our Simple, Secure 4-Step Process

Step 1: Submit Your Quote Request

Use our simple online form to tell us about your items. Upload a few clear photos—no need for a professional camera, your phone is perfect.

Step 2: Receive Your Offer

Shawn will personally assess your items and send you a transparent, top-dollar offer based on real-time market values. No obligations, no pressure.

Step 3: Arrange Secure Handover

If you accept the offer, we make it easy. Choose a secure, insured, prepaid shipping label on us, or schedule a safe, public meetup in the Pittsburgh area.

Step 4: Get Paid Instantly

As soon as your items are verified, we send your payment immediately through your preferred method—Cash, Zelle, Venmo, or Check.
